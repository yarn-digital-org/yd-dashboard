# Yarn Digital Ad Creative Style Guide

Based on existing creatives in Google Drive folder: `1mUeQ8wKVrKX_jcvlZ09XMFEzbNEOm_AD`

## Overview
Dark, minimal, typographically bold. Premium agency aesthetic — no photos, no illustrations. Just type, space, and subtle geometric accents.

## Color Palette
- **Background:** Near-black (#0D0D0D – #111111)
- **Primary text:** White (#FFFFFF)
- **Secondary text:** Muted grey (#8A8A8A – #999999)
- **Accent:** Red-orange (#E8420A – #FF4500) — CTA button, logo dot, decorative elements ONLY
- **Decorative:** Dark red-brown (#5C1A0A – #7A2010) — low-opacity accent shapes

## Typography
- **Headlines:** Geometric sans-serif, Black/900 weight, white, large, tight leading (~1.0-1.1x)
- **Body:** Same family, Regular/Light weight, muted grey
- **Logo:** "YARN" (bold caps, white) + red-orange dot + "Digital" (light, white)

## Layout (1080×1350, 4:5)
- Left-aligned throughout
- Generous top padding (~25-30% empty)
- Logo above headline
- CTA pill button bottom-left (red-orange, "Get a Free Audit →")
- URL bottom-right (grey, lowercase)

## Decorative Elements
- 1-2 per ad max
- Thin-stroke geometric shapes: circles, corners, bars, lines
- Always in red-orange accent at low opacity
- Positioned asymmetrically (upper-right, lower-left)
- Never compete with headline

## Copy Style
- Short, punchy, provocative
- Pain-point driven ("Your website is losing you customers")
- Conversational and confrontational
- Period-heavy punctuation

## Asset Types in Drive
- Brand Carousel (White + Dark variants)
- Ad Carousel (White + Dark variants)
- Service Carousel (White + Dark variants)
- Both Feed (1080x1350) and Stories (1080x1920) sizes
