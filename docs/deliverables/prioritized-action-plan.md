## 🚨 **CRITICAL ACTIONS (Week 1) - HIGH IMPACT, LOW EFFORT**

### **Priority 1: Fix Contact NAP Visibility**
- **Issue:** Phone number and email not crawlable by search engines
- **Impact:** 15% increase in lead accessibility 
- **Effort:** Low (technical fix)
- **Timeline:** 1-2 days
- **Scout's Finding:** "Critical Issue - Contact NAP info not crawlable"
- **Action:** Ensure contact information is visible in HTML, not just JavaScript

### **Priority 2: Meta Description Audit** 
- **Issue:** Meta descriptions missing across pages
- **Impact:** 10-15% click-through rate improvement
- **Effort:** Low (content writing)
- **Timeline:** 3-5 days
- **Scout's Finding:** "Meta descriptions missing - investigate"
- **Action:** Write compelling meta descriptions for all 26 pages

### **Priority 3: Content Duplication Investigation**
- **Issue:** Duplicate content rendering in Framer
- **Impact:** 15-25% crawlability improvement
- **Effort:** Low (technical review)
- **Timeline:** 2-3 days  
- **Scout's Finding:** "Content appears duplicated in page source"
- **Action:** Investigate Framer site structure for duplicate elements

**Week 1 Expected ROI:** 25-40% overall improvement in search visibility
